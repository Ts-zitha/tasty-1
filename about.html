The Restaurant

The Tasty is located on 198 West 21th Street, in the heart of Nkomazi, For the past 30 years, The Tasty Restaurant has provided its guests with an exceptional dining experience. With management always on hand to attend to customers individual needs and food prepared to the highest of standards in an enjoyable atmosphere, we are fortunate to truly enjoy what we do.Our dishes are all freshly, hygienically, and individually prepared to meet our own, and your, high standards..
